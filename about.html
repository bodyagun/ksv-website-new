About KSV

BUILT ON THE MAT.

KSV Performance Gear started in the wrestling room and never left it.

KSV was founded by competitors who got tired of gear that looked good on a hanger and fell apart in live training. Every piece we make gets tested the same way it'll be used: under grips, under sweat, under pressure.

Today that means singlets and training tees for the wrestling room, rash guards and fight shorts for the cage, and official-style kits for national squads who compete in our gear on the world stage.

No filler. No flash. Just gear built for the people who show up to train when no one's watching.
